Capacity guide

How to read this page

Use this as a quick reference for the scenario buttons, hour tiles, trickle arrows, and the detailed hour modal.

Core idea

Every hour is a supply-vs-demand comparison. Supply comes from doctors scheduled in that hour. Demand comes from the matching prior-week hour, adjusted by the selected distribution scenario.

Blue = supply
Green = demand
Percent = coverage

Distribution Scenarios

Changes demand, supply, and trickle display
Worst
High pressure
base + max(queue signals)

Uses the strongest queue pressure available, such as inherited backlog, trickle added, or inferred total-over-base demand.

Normal
Expected
base + trickle_added

Keeps the real trickle component in the hour instead of removing queue effects entirely.

Best
Lower pressure
base + trickle_added - dropped

Assumes some queued work drops out when that column is available; otherwise it falls back to multiplier-based scaling.

Hour Tiles And Trickle Arrows

Tile numbers scheduled supply vs expected demand

Hovering a tile shows the capacity ratio percentage.

Arrows queue RVUs entering the next hour

The arrow number changes with the distribution mode because the queue component is scenario-adjusted.

0-2: low 2-5: building 5-10: heavy 10+: critical

Sorting And Filters

1

Direction reorders the visible hour cards by time. It does not change any calculations.

2

State and facility filters highlight matching hours based on who is licensed or assigned.

3

Calendar navigation preserves the selected distribution when you move between weeks.

Inside The Hour Modal

Allocation detail

The modal breaks demand into state and modality groups, then allocates each scheduled doctor's RVUs based on license coverage and modality fit.

RVUs vs Cases

This only changes display units. Case counts are derived from modality chunks, so the underlying allocation remains the same.

Modal distribution menu

Changing the scenario inside the modal refetches that hour and recalculates demand, queue multiplier, supply, and doctor allocations.

What-if base RVUs

Enter temporary doctor RVU overrides, then recalculate. Overrides are not saved and reset when the hour is reopened.
